About the Destination

Kedarnath is a divine Hindu pilgrimage destination where thousands of tourists and pilgrims come every year to seek the blessings of Lord Shiva. It is famous for the Kedarnath shrine, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, set against the majestic Kedarnath range.

Trek is serene with rhododendron forests along the Himalayan paths. Summer is pleasant; winter is harsh.
